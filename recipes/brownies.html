Recipe Description

This easy brownie recipe takes about 30 minutes to make from start to finish for a chocolaty treat!

Bake these easy brownies whenever you need to quickly satisfy your sweet tooth. Made with kitchen staples, you'll come back to this easy brownie recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup walnut halves
